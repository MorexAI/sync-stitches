FAQ

Common questions.

Is StitchSync for brands or consumers?
Both. App users manage fit passports, while manufacturers use structured fit signals.
Do I need to guess my clothing size?
No. StitchSync focuses on measurements and fit results rather than manual size selection.
Can I change or update my measurements?
Yes. Update your measurements anytime in the dashboard to refresh fit recommendations.
